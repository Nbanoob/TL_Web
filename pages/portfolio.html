Zombie Cartastrophe

Zombie Cartastrophe is a game where the player takes control of a weaponized car and has to defeat waves of zombies. The player gets money for killing zombies and can spend that money on the store to upgrade their arsenal. The game was made with a game engine called Construct 2 and with a team of 3 people, including myself.

Lead developer of the project. Mainly contributed to the technical side of the game and not the creative side. The game was created in 2016.

In-Development Discord Bot

Over the summer, I picked up learning how to develop a bot for the communication platform Discord. It started out as a joke between friends, but it quickly developed into a genuine interest of the Discord API that I used to create the bot. The bot currently has a lot of inside jokes and is very basic, but the most practical uses of the bot is that it can delete messages, whether it be its own or others. It can distinguish between its own messages and others to know what to delete.

I plan to add more features to the bot so that it can be a more focused bot. As it stands, it doesn't serve much of a purpose other than being a little entertaining for my friends and I.
